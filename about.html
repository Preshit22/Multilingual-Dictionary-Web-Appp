About Dictionary App

This Dictionary App is a full-stack web application created to help users find word definitions effortlessly. It leverages the power of the OpenAI GPT-3.5 Turbo model to deliver accurate and detailed definitions. The app was developed by Kshitij Sarve, Preshit Desai, and Om Patange, aiming to provide a useful and intuitive tool for anyone seeking to enhance their vocabulary.
